💻 Apple and Microsoft hike prices as the AI memory crunch bites🆕 New🆓 Free

The AI boom just showed up on the price tag. Apple raised Mac and iPad prices Thursday, many by 20%+: the base MacBook Air went $1,099→$1,299, the cheapest MacBook Pro $1,699→$1,999, iPad Air $599→$749 and iPad Pro $999→$1,199. Microsoft will lift Xbox Series X/S prices $100–$150 from Aug 1. The culprit is a global DRAM squeeze — memory prices are up ~98% in 2026 as AI data centers hoover up chips. The iPhone was spared for now, but analysts expect richer pricing when the iPhone 18 lands in September.

🏦 Social Security's trustees: reserves run dry in 2032, then a ~24% cut🆕 New🆓 Free

The 2026 Trustees Report pegs the retirement trust fund's depletion at Q4 2032; after that, payroll taxes would cover only ~78% of scheduled benefits — about a $500/month hit for the average retiree absent a fix. Nothing changes overnight, and Congress has levers (higher taxes, trimmed benefits, or both), but it's a real reason for anyone near retirement to stress-test their plan rather than assume the status quo.
