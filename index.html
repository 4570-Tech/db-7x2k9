📉 Social Security trust fund now projected to run dry in late 2032🆓 Free

The 2026 Trustees Report moved the depletion date up to late 2032 — about six years out. Not a reason to claim early in a panic, but a real nudge to stress-test your plan against a haircut. Note: Full Retirement Age also officially reaches 67 this November.

⚖️ SCOTUS hands Trump a TPS win — courts can't second-guess most terminations🆕 New🆓 Free

In Mullin v. Doe (consolidated with Trump v. Miot), the Court ruled 6–3 Thursday that the TPS statute bars courts from reviewing most challenges to ending a country's designation — lifting lower-court holds that had paused terminations for an estimated 350,000 Haitians and ~6,000 Syrians.
